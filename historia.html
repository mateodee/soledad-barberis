Mi Historia

I was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, and I currently have 51 years old. I have been working in a company for more than 20 years, in an administrative position. By chance and due to the 2020 pandemic I had the opportunity to attend a virtual collage course to get out of the routine, I wanted to do something creative that always attracted me, to connect a little with my emotions. I didn’t have to take so many classes to realize that contact with paper and scissors was familiar to me, it transported me to a magical place with memories of my childhood. I remembered how much I liked to cut and paste as a child, and today has become a type of therapy for me. I enjoy it a lot, it calms me down, it relaxes me, and it became almost a necessity for me. Through the images that I choose, I am able to express my attraction by the bright colors, flowers, textures, characters, and transport myself into a dream to an inner universe were I go every time I need to find my true self.
